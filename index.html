About the studio

Small studio, broad mobile portfolio.

Extrude Gaming Studio is an independent developer creating mobile titles for Android, iOS and Amazon devices. The portfolio spans superheroes, open-world city missions, racing, survival, kids learning, tycoon systems and casual ASMR play.

The studio focus is direct: approachable controls, strong visual hooks and games that run well on a wide range of phones and tablets.
