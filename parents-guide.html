Parents Guide

Extrude Gaming Studio publishes mobile games across action, racing, casual and simulation genres. Parents can use device and store settings to manage downloads, play time, purchases and ads.

Parent tips

  • Review each game store page before downloading.
  • Use Google Play, App Store or Amazon parental controls for purchases and downloads.
  • Check device-level screen time and privacy settings.
  • Contact support if you need help with a specific game.

Privacy and ads

Some games may use ads or third-party services. Review the Privacy Policy for more details.
